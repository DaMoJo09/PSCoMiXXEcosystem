## 5. Creation Modes (All Must Be Preserved & Polished)

### 5.1 Comic Builder (Image + Video + Drawing)

Panels & Layouts:

* Common panel templates (1‚Äì4, grids, manga layouts).
* Ability to change layouts mid-project without breaking content too badly.

Per-panel content types:

1. **Image**

   * Backgrounds from library/uploads.

2. **Drawing Layer**

   * Brush/eraser, color, size, opacity.
   * Stylus/Wacom support where possible.
   * Inline drawing mode vs full-blank mode (fixed as per `CRITICAL_FIXES.md`).

3. **Video**

   * Drop in video clips into panels (like in current Manus prototype).
   * Basic control: start/end, autoplay flag for reader.
   * Text/bubbles overlay on top.

4. **Text & Bubbles**

   * Speech bubbles, captions, SFX text.
   * Multiple fonts, size, alignment, outline/shadow.
   * Drag/resize/rotate.

Canvas behavior:

* Drag/resize/rotate elements.
* Snap guides.
* Zoom + pan.
* Robust undo/redo.

### 5.2 Cover Creator

* Dedicated cover mode:

  * Hero art (image or composition).
  * Title, subtitle, author text.
  * Logo/badge.
  * QR code (linking out to psstreaming/pscomixx).

* Live preview (no delayed updates).

* Export:

  * Print-ready PNG/JPEG.
  * Web preview.

### 5.3 Trading Card Creator (Evolution-Ready)

* Card templates (by rarity/frame).

* Fields: name, art, stats, rarity, flavor text, type/tags.

* Card evolution:

  * Structure for multiple stages (base ‚Üí advanced ‚Üí legendary).
  * Shared card ID; different visuals/stats per stage.

* Use `CARD_FILE_FORMAT.md` for `.card` I/O.

* Export:

  * High-res PNG for print.
  * Web-size preview.

### 5.4 Visual Novel Creator (Flagship)

* Project structure:

  * Scenes (nodes).
  * Characters (with sprite sets).
  * Backgrounds.
  * Dialogue lines.
  * Choices/branches.

* Scene editor:

  * Background per scene.
  * List or graph of scenes.
  * Sequence of dialogue entries.

* Dialogue editor:

  * Speaker, text, sprite/pose, position (L/C/R).
  * Add/duplicate/reorder lines.
  * In-app playtest.

* Characters:

  * Character library with name/color.
  * Multiple expression sprites.

* Choices & branching:

  * At certain lines, present choices.
  * Each choice links to another scene or point.
  * Some form of node/graph visualization is ideal.

* Optional future variables:

  * Flags/variables with simple conditions.
  * Design data model to allow this later.

* Exports:

  * JSON of full VN (scenes, dialogue, choices, characters, backgrounds).
  * Optional asset bundle ZIP.
  * Internal ‚ÄúPlay‚Äù mode for testing.
